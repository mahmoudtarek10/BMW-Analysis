🚗 BMW Analysis (Power BI)

This project is a fully interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze and visualize BMW sales performance across multiple dimensions, including revenue trends, sales volume, customer markets, and product segmentation.

The dashboard transforms raw data into actionable insights, enabling better business decision-making through clear, dynamic, and user-friendly visualizations.

📊 Key Features

Revenue Analysis

Total Revenue vs Previous Year comparison

Year-over-Year performance tracking (2019–2023)

Growth indicators and trend visualization

Sales Performance

Top-selling car models (e.g., BMW series)

Total quantity sold and growth rates

Country-level sales breakdown (Egypt, France, USA)

Product Insights

Top expensive car categories

Model-level performance comparison

Channel Analysis

Revenue distribution across:

Wholesale

Dealership

Online

Interactive donut chart for channel contribution

Time-Based Insights

Yearly comparison of sales quantity vs previous year

Trend analysis for long-term performance
